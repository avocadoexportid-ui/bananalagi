Our Premium Varieties

We export three distinct banana varieties, each selected for their unique taste, texture, and market demand.

Dessert Banana

Cavendish

The world's most popular banana. Known for its creamy texture, sweetness, and long shelf life. Ideal for fresh retail markets.

Grade: Class A
Size: Minimum 19cm
Packaging: 13.5kg/18.5kg Box
Cooking Banana

Kepok (Saba)

A starchy, robust banana perfect for cooking. Widely used in traditional dishes, chips, and flour production. High demand in Asian markets.

Grade: Premium
Texture: Firm & Starchy
Usage: Culinary/Processing
Processing / Snack

Tanduk (Horn Banana)

Recognized by its horn-like shape and large size. Offers a unique aroma and excellent texture for banana fritters and industrial processing.

Grade: Select
Size: Extra Large
Flavor: Distinct Aroma

About BananaIndonesia

Rooted in Indonesia’s rich volcanic soil, Banana Indonesia has been a trusted bridge between local farmers and the global market for over a decade.

We believe in empowering local agriculture while delivering the freshest, highest-quality tropical fruits to the world. Our commitment to sustainable farming and fair trade ensures that every banana we export benefits the community and the environment.
